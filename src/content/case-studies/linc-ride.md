## The Situation

LincRide needed to grow their user base — both drivers and riders — in a competitive market. Their paid campaigns were live, but the team couldn't clearly trace which ads drove sign-ups, which spend was wasted, and where leads dropped off after clicking.

The result: budget burn without a clear picture of return.

## What We Did

We built a connected acquisition system from ad click to CRM record:

- **Meta Ads restructure:** Rebuilt campaign architecture with proper audience segmentation, funnel-matched creative, and conversion-event tracking.
- **Attribution pipeline:** Wired Meta Pixel and GA4 together so every click, sign-up, and conversion had a source attached to it.
- **CRM integration:** Connected inbound leads to Zoho CRM automatically — no manual data entry, no lost contacts.
- **Automated follow-up:** Built onboarding sequences triggered by CRM entry, reducing time-to-activation for new driver sign-ups.

## The Results

- **590% ROI** on managed campaign spend over the engagement period
- **7M+ impressions** managed across the program
- Full attribution from ad click to CRM record, for the first time
- Reduction in cost per acquisition through continuous creative and audience testing
