## The work no one should be doing

Every business has processes that run the same way, every day or every week, handled by a person who deserves to be doing something more valuable.

The invoice that gets manually copied from one system to another. The daily report that someone assembles from three different spreadsheets. The customer inquiry that has to be manually logged in the CRM. The approval request that sits in email because no one built a better way.

This work isn't just a waste of time — it introduces errors, creates bottlenecks, and burns out the people doing it.

## What we build

We design and build automated workflows that replace manual, repetitive steps with systems that run themselves:

- **Data sync and integration** — connect your CRM, accounting, ops, and communication tools so data moves automatically
- **Approval and notification flows** — route requests, trigger alerts, and escalate automatically based on conditions
- **Report generation** — scheduled, data-pulled reports delivered to the right people without manual effort
- **Lead and customer workflows** — capture, assign, and follow up on leads without anyone touching a keyboard
- **Internal operations** — HR processes, onboarding checklists, task creation from triggers

## How we do it

We use **n8n** as our primary automation platform — it's self-hostable, powerful, and doesn't lock you into a per-task pricing model that scales against you. For simpler integrations or specific tool combinations, we'll use whatever is the right tool for the job.

We design the workflow with you first, map every step and exception, then build and test before anything touches your live systems.
