## Why most CRM projects go wrong

The CRM gets purchased. Someone in IT or management sets it up. The sales team gets a login. Three months later, half the records are incomplete, no one is logging activities, and leadership can't trust the pipeline report.

This isn't a people problem. It's a configuration and adoption problem.

A CRM works when it mirrors how your team actually sells — when the stages match your process, when the fields are the ones people actually know, when data entry is minimal because half of it is automated.

## What we do

**Discovery and process mapping**
Before touching the software, we document your actual sales or customer management process. Where do leads come from? What happens at each stage? What information does your team need to move a deal forward?

**Configuration and customisation**
We build the CRM around your process: custom pipeline stages, relevant fields, views your team will actually use, automation rules that keep records current.

**Integration**
Connect your CRM to the tools your team uses daily — WhatsApp, email, your website's contact form, your accounting software. Data flows in automatically so manual entry is minimal.

**Training and adoption**
We don't hand over a login and a manual. We run practical training with your team, built around the specific workflows they'll use every day.

**Ongoing support**
As your process evolves, we're available to adjust the configuration, add new automations, and extend the system.

## Platforms we work with
