## The data problem most businesses have

You have more data than you know what to do with, and less visibility than you need.

Your CRM has customer records. Your ad platform has spend and conversion data. Your accounting software has revenue. Your ops tool has pipeline status. None of them talk to each other, and no one has the time or the tools to connect them manually.

So decisions get made on incomplete pictures. Budget goes to campaigns that look good in isolation but aren't driving revenue. Sales teams don't know which leads to prioritise. Leadership asks "how are we doing?" and the answer takes three days to prepare.

## What we build

**Centralised dashboards**
One place where leadership can see the full business: revenue, pipeline, lead volume, campaign performance, and operational metrics — updated in real time or on a schedule that makes sense.

**Marketing attribution**
Connect your ad spend to actual revenue outcomes. Know which channels, campaigns, and creatives are driving customers — not just clicks.

**Sales pipeline reporting**
Where are deals in the funnel? Where are they stalling? Which reps are closing and which need support? Answerable in seconds, not days.

**Automated reports**
Weekly performance summaries, monthly P&L snapshots, campaign reports — delivered to the right inboxes automatically, no manual assembly.

**Data integration and ETL**
Pull data from CRMs, ad platforms, spreadsheets, and APIs into a single warehouse or reporting layer so your dashboards always have fresh, accurate data.

## What you walk away with

A data system that works for your business, not a generic dashboard with metrics no one looks at. We design for the decisions that actually need to be made — then build the infrastructure to support them.
